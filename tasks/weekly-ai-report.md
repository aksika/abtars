# Weekly AI Report

Read all emails from the last 7 days (aksikatwo@gmail.com), filter for AI-relevant newsletters, and generate a weekly report.

Follow the AI Weekly Brief format with sections: Top Stories, Research, Products & Launches, Policy & Regulation, Open Source, Funding & Deals.

Write the report to ~/.agentbridge/reports/AI-Weekly-{today}.md. Send the report to the chat when done.

## Definition of Done
- ~/.agentbridge/reports/AI-Weekly-{today}.md
